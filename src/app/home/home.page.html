Welcome to UALCAN Mobile, a free app available in android and iOS/Apple app store for analyzing gene expression, proteomics and promoter methylation data.

It is a companion app for the web based UALCAN and provides easy access to and analysis of TCGA gene expression and promoter methylation data as well as CPTAC proteomics data.
